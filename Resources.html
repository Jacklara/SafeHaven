Resources and Services

We are here to help! We offer a variety of things to help victims and potential victims to help combat, and prevent on-going and future cases of human trafficking.

Safehouses

Safehouses are local, convenient, safe, and well guarded shelters available 24/7 for any and all trafficking victims in need of sanctuary. With up to 6 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms and accessed exclusivly through a check in feature on this site. If you have any questions please refer to our contact page or you need access CLICK HERE

Self-Defense Classes

Self-defense is an essential part of keeping yourself and loved ones protected from potentially dangerous situations. We offer 20%-40% off discounts to local well respected martial arts schools with programs teaching Krav maga, Jiu Jitsu, Kickboxing, etc. all around the charlotte area. If you have any interest and want to learn to become disiplined, confident and have greater peace of mind we recommend this Krav Maga School.

Legal Counseling

If you are in need of legal assistance, we work with Snow Legal's Mecklenburg Criminal Defense Law Firm which empowers women, men, and children to seek justice. They lead national efforts to hold human traffikers accountable for their crimes and to raise awareness of victim's rights throughout North Carolina. For more information about legal assistance visit Snow Legal's site.

Support Groups

We collaborate with other widely known organizations in North Carolina with the similar goal of preventing females, males, and children from falling victim to human trafficking, and offering assistanceto those still trapped, and restore those who have survived by offering weekly support group meetings held at our local facilities in the Charlotte area to offer hope, empowerment, and a brighter future. For more information read over this list of partners.
