Why Get Invovled?

Charlotte has the highest rate of sex trafficking in the state of North Carolina. Unsuspecting individuals are pulled from their families and homes each day. Get invovled with our community by donating to our cause. You can donate to a GoFundMe that aids survivors back to their normal day lives. You can also donate personal clothing or food at the locations listed under. Anything helps in these situations. We as a city need to come together to stop this growing issue at hand!
